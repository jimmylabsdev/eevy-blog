Section 01 · EV HOME CHARGING CHALLENGES

How To Avoid EV Charging Problems Before It's Too Late

Many EV buyers discover charging challenges only after booking their vehicle. Here are some of the most common issues that can delay or complicate home charging.

  • Do I Need Additional Load Sanctioned?
    Your existing electrical connection may not support the charger speed you expect.
  • What About Permissions & Approvals?
    Some apartments require permissions, NOCs or committee approvals before a charger can be installed.
  • How Expensive Is the Installation?
    Wiring, civil work and electrical upgrades can increase installation costs beyond the charger itself.
  • Is My Parking Space Suitable For EV Charging?
    The distance between your parking space and electrical connection can significantly affect installation complexity and cost.

SECTION 03 · EV CHARGER COSTS

Don't Get Blindsided - Know All The Charges Beforehand

Many EV buyers budget for the charger, but overlook the hidden costs of civil work, wiring, and electrical upgrades.

  • Do I have to pay extra for the charger unit?
    Yes. Unless your dealer is running an offer, the charger unit can alone cost you upwards of ₹50k plus installation costs.
  • Are there any additional charges for the Home Charger?
    Depending on your designated parking spot and the distance from the meter room, you may have to bear the cost for extra wiring, conduits, and upgrades.
  • Why do I need Society's approval?
    Your Society may allow or reject running additional lines in the basement from the meter room for charger installation.
  • Who Pays For Wiring And Civil Work?
    The charger is only one part of the setup. Wiring, conduits, drilling and electrical modifications can add to the final bill.
