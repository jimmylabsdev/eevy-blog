Home Charging vs Highway Charging

Apr 25, 2026

For most Indian EV owners, 80–90% of charging happens at home. Understanding when to use what type of charger saves money and time.

Home AC charging

A 3.3 kW or 7.4 kW AC charger installed at home will fully charge most EVs overnight. Cheap, convenient, and easy on the battery.

Highway DC fast charging

Useful only on long trips. Charges from 20% to 80% in 30–45 minutes on a 50 kW unit, but costs 3–4x more per unit than home charging.
