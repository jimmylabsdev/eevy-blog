EV vs Petrol: Real Cost per km in India (2026)

Apr 27, 2026

The honest answer to “is an EV cheaper to run?” depends on where you live, how you charge, and how much you drive. Let’s walk through the numbers.

The basics

A typical petrol hatchback in India delivers around 18–20 km/l of mileage. At ~₹105/litre, that’s roughly ₹5.5 per km.

An EV with a real-world efficiency of 7 km/kWh, charged at ₹8/kWh on a home tariff, comes in at about ₹1.15 per km.

What changes the math

Public DC fast charging can push EV running cost up to ₹2.5–3.5 per km. Home charging keeps it cheap. Battery degradation, tyre wear and insurance differences also matter — but on running cost alone, the gap is real.
